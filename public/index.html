What it does

From the kitchen to the inspection report

Menus & ordering

Browse stalls, order dishes across cuisines, and keep every order in one cart and history.

Feedback & complaints

Ratings, comments and complaints route straight to the right stall, giving a clear satisfaction view.

Hygiene & inspections

Officers log scores and remarks; each stall's full history of hygiene grades stays transparent.

Who it's for

Built for everyone in the hawker ecosystem

Patrons

Browse, order and review.

Stall owners

Manage menus and respond to feedback.

Operators

Track rentals and stall performance.

NEA officers

Inspect, grade and keep records.
